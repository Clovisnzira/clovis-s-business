NEW FASHION GENERATION BUSINESS(NFGB)

BACKGROUND OF NEW FASHION GENERATION BUSINESS

    New Fashion Generation Business was born on the 24th of October 2019, in Rwanda Country, Eastern province precisely in KIREHE District, to bring better health to the people through nutrition, clothing, and access to affordable medicine based on human capacity.
    Since its creation in 2019 by Clovis NZIRAGUHINDWA, New Fashion Generation Business is committed to helping orphans, widows, the elderly, children of on the street, children in prison, and children with various problems and other people who have disabilities.

The main products produced by New Fashion Generation Business(NFGB)are the following:

A. For Nutrition: We produce the nutrients which are categorized into seven main classes of nutrients that our body needs. Those classes are: Carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals, fibre and water and other drinks such as Fanta, Juice, Beer, and so on. B. For Clothes: We produce the clothes for all people such as: Trousers, shirts-shirts, shorts and shoes for men and boys. In addition to that, we produce Trousers, underwear, t-shirts, shirts, shoes and pullovers for women and girls. C.
